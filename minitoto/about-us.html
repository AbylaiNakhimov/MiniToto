Some information about
Mini Toto store

“Mini-Toto” is a clothing store created with the aim of selling clothes exclusively for children. The store has been opened in 2019.
For two years, we had worked only through the social media page, where we regularly posted photos with products to attract an audience.
The store is currently selling products of brands such as "Gioseppo", "CC Smyk", "Artie", "Serinity", "Emporio Armani", "Carter's" and others.

The purchase was made through direct communication with the owner via ‘Direct’ or ‘WhatsApp’, the price of the product was announced in the description under the post. Two years later, in 2021, the store announced the opening.
